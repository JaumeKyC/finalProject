Bill Gates

Bill Gates is an American entrepreneur and software developer born in Seattle in 1955. He co-founded Microsoft Corporation in 1975, which became the world's largest personal-computer software company.

Gates is widely recognized as a pioneer of the personal computer revolution and has been instrumental in transforming the way we work and live. He is also a philanthropist and has donated billions of dollars to charity through the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation, which he founded with his wife in 2000.

Despite stepping down from his leadership role at Microsoft in 2008, Gates remains an influential figure in technology and global health initiatives.
